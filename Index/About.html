About Us

Widely known for customer centricity, the Bank has gone through the various phases of its growth trajectory over hundred years of its existence. Growth of the Bank was phenomenal, especially after nationalization in the year 1969, attaining the status of a national level player in terms of geographical reach and clientele segments.
Eighties was characterized by business diversification for the Bank.

In June 2006, the Bank completed a century of operation in the Indian banking industry. The eventful journey of the Bank has been characterized by several memorable milestones. Today, Our Bank occupies a premier position in the comity of Indian banks.
